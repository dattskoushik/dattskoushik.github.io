About Me

I am a Software engineer - Backend and Data by mistake but later developed with a passion for building systems, pipelines, and infrastructure that are maintenance-friendly, reliable, and performant.

With around 5 years of experience in production environments, I specialize in designing data pipelines that handle complexity with grace. My work focuses on the intersection of data engineering and backend infrastructure, ensuring that data is not just moved, but understood and delivered reliably.

I value code clarity, good relatable design, and understandable documentation. I believe that great engineering is more on boring side, because it just works based on what you write.

My journey has taken me through a variety of domains, from building ETL workflows and distributed systems to optimizing SQL queries and deploying scalable microservices. I enjoy collaborating with cross-functional teams, mentoring junior engineers, and continuously learning new technologies to stay ahead in a rapidly evolving field.

Outside of work, I am passionate about sharing knowledge through technical writing, open-source contributions, and speaking at meetups. I believe in the power of community and strive to make technology accessible and impactful for everyone.

My Philosophy

I write code that works and is easy to understand. I focus on solving problems simply rather than overcomplicating things.

I enjoy working through hard problems and learning from them. I like to keep things straightforward and practical.

I work best as a individual contributer and at times with teams too. I value clear communication and different viewpoints. I believe in creating an environment where people can share ideas openly.
